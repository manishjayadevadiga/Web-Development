Manish's Recipe for the Best Maggie

Ingredients

Essential:

  • 1 bowl water
  • gas and gas stove
  • maggie
  • masala

For fillings

  • Spring onions
  • Carrots
  • Green Peas

Instructions

  1. On the gas and keep wate rto boil
  2. After the water is boiled add maggie noodles
  3. Now add maggie masala and mix it
  4. After it is cooked add vegetables
  5. Keep it for 2-3 mins and serve in a clean plate
